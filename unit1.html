Your First 15 Gurmukhi Letters 🎉

These are the first 15 letters of the Gurmukhi alphabet. In this unit we learn each letter in its mukta form — the base sound with no extra vowel signs attached. Master these and you are on your way!

💡 Mukta means the letter is read with its natural short "uh" sound — no matra (vowel sign) added. For example ਕ = "kuh", ਗ = "guh", ਜ = "juh".
