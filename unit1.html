🪄 Two Magic Marks to Know!

Some words below wear special marks. Spot them on top of letters and remember what they do!

ੰ
Tippi — The Nose Buzz
Curves down like a little tunnel 🌀
Makes you hum through your nose — like "sing"
ੱ
Adhak — The Bouncing Ball
Curves up like a smile 😊
Makes your voice bounce hard on the next sound — like "butter"

💡 Tip: See a mark curving down? Hum through your nose. See one curving up? Give the next sound an extra bounce!
