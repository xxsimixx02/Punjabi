🪄 Magic Dot Marks!

Some words below have special marks called Tippi (ੰ) and Bindi (ਂ). They give letters a "nose voice" — a gentle buzzing sound through your nose, like the "ng" in "sing" or the "m" in "jump". Look out for the little dot on top!
