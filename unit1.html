First 20 Gurmukhi Letters 🔤

These are the first 20 letters of the Gurmukhi alphabet in their mukta form — the base sound with no vowel signs added.

💡 Mukta means the letter is read with its natural short sound — no matra added. For example ਕ = "kuh", ਗ = "guh", ਜ = "juh".
